That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime

That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime ｢転生したらスライムだった件, tensei shitara suraimu datta ken, lit. "Regarding Reincarnated to Slime"｣, abbreviated as TenSura ｢転スラ, tensura｣, is a light novel series written by Fuse and illustrated by Mitz Vah. It originally started as a web novel hosted on Shōsetsuka ni Narō between 2013-2016. The series was acquired by Micro Magazine, who published the first light novel volume in 2014.
